TeraWatt Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Overview

TeraWatt is an offline puzzle game developed by Keith ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app does — and does not — collect.

Data Collection

TeraWatt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No account or sign-in is required.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs are included.
  • No advertising networks are used.
  • The app does not request or use INTERNET permission.

Local Data

All game data — including puzzle progress, scores, settings, and cosmetic purchases — is stored locally on your device using Android's standard SharedPreferences and Room database. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

TeraWatt does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. The app functions entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

TeraWatt does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
